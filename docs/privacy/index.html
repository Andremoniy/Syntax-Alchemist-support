Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Syntax Alchemist handles information.

Last updated: 2 May 2026

Overview

Syntax Alchemist is designed as a local, account-free puzzle game. The app does not require registration, does not display advertising, and does not use third-party analytics services.

Information we collect

Syntax Alchemist does not collect personal information directly from you. The app does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access.

Gameplay progress, settings, completed levels, help credit balance, and similar app data are stored locally on your device.

In-app purchases

Syntax Alchemist may offer optional in-app purchases for help credits. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

We do not receive or store your payment card details. Apple may provide purchase-related information required for the app to unlock or deliver purchased content.

Diagnostics and crash information

If you have enabled sharing diagnostics with app developers in your Apple device settings, Apple may provide crash reports or diagnostic information through Apple developer tools.

This information is used only to understand and fix technical problems.

Third-party services

Syntax Alchemist does not use third-party advertising networks, tracking SDKs, or analytics SDKs.

The app relies on Apple services for App Store distribution and in-app purchases.

Children’s privacy

Syntax Alchemist does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not require an account and does not collect personal details directly, no personal profile is created for any player.

Data retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device unless you delete it, reset the app, or remove the app from your device.

Consumable help credits that have already been used cannot be restored after deletion or reset.

Your choices

  • You can delete local app data by deleting the app from your device.
  • You can manage App Store purchases and subscriptions, where applicable, through your Apple ID settings.
  • You can control diagnostic sharing in your iOS privacy settings.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date.
