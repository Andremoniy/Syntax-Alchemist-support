About the app

Syntax Alchemist is a logic puzzle game where you rewrite syntax trees by applying reactions. Each level gives you a starting tree, a set of available rules, and a target result.

The challenge is to find the right sequence of transformations while handling special nodes, obstacles, limited moves, and optional bonuses.

Help credits

If you get stuck, you can use help credits to reveal the next move. A small number of help credits is available at the beginning.

More help credits can be purchased as optional in-app purchases. You can continue playing without buying credits, but hints may be limited.

Help credits are consumable items. Used credits cannot be restored after they have been spent.

Purchase troubleshooting

  • Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase.
  • Check that your internet connection is active.
  • Close and reopen the app after a completed purchase.
  • If a purchase was charged but credits did not appear, contact support with the approximate purchase time.

FAQ

What is the goal of each level?

Each level asks you to transform the starting tree into the required target. Some levels require an exact final tree, while others introduce special conditions.

What are reactions?

Reactions are rewrite rules. Applying a reaction changes part of the current tree and moves you closer to, or sometimes further from, the solution.

Can I play without hints?

Yes. Hints are optional. They are there only to help when a puzzle becomes too difficult.

Can I restore purchased help credits?

Help credits are consumable in-app purchases. Credits that have already been used cannot be restored. If a completed purchase did not add credits to your balance, contact support.

The app is not working correctly. What should I send?

Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, a short description of the problem, and screenshots if they help explain the issue.

Privacy

Syntax Alchemist does not require an account to play. Gameplay progress and help credit balance are stored locally on your device.
